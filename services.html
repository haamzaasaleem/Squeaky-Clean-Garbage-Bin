Everything we'll scrub, sanitize & spray

Whether it's one funky can or a whole driveway, here's exactly what we do — and what it costs. No surprises, no hidden fees.

Garbage Bin Cleaning

Inside and out, top to wheels. Our truck-mounted system hits each cart with 200°+ pressurized water to break down stuck-on residue most hoses never touch.

Sanitizing & Disinfecting

Heat plus an eco-friendly sanitizer eliminates up to 99.9% of bacteria — E. coli, salmonella, listeria — the stuff that quietly makes bins a health hazard.

Deodorizing

Every visit ends with a fresh-scent finish, so the lid-lift no longer comes with a wince. Especially clutch during valley summers.

Pressure Washing

Driveways, sidewalks, patios, steps, garage floors and trash enclosures. We lift oil, algae and grime and brighten the whole approach to your home.

Commercial & HOA

Dumpsters, enclosures, loading docks and multi-unit cart routes. Keep tenants happy and stay ahead of code — ask about neighborhood group rates.

Eco Wastewater Handling

We capture and filter our rinse water instead of letting it run into the gutter — better for Fresno's storm drains and fully EPA-conscious.

Weekly · Bi-weekly · Monthly

Not sure how often you need it? Here's the rule of thumb.

It mostly comes down to what goes in the can and how hot it's been outside.

  • Weekly — lots of food waste, pets, diapers, or a big household. Cans never get a chance to turn.
  • Bi-weekly — our most popular pick. Keeps odors and bugs away for a typical family without overdoing it.
  • Monthly — smaller households or mostly dry trash that just needs a regular reset.
  • One-time — post-party, post-move, or "I can't believe I let it get this bad" deep cleans.

Good questions

Before you book

Do I need to be home?

Nope. Just leave your empty cans at the curb on your scheduled day after the garbage truck comes. We clean them and put them back.

Do you use my water?

No — we bring our own and we capture the dirty rinse water so nothing runs into your gutter or storm drain.

What if my city picks up on a different day?

Tell us your collection day when you book and we'll line up our route to hit your cans right after pickup, while they're empty.

Is there a contract?

None. Recurring plans are month-to-month — pause, skip a visit, or cancel whenever you need to.

Which areas do you serve?

Fresno, Clovis and the surrounding Central Valley communities. Not sure if you're in range? Call 559-317-5916 and we'll let you know.
