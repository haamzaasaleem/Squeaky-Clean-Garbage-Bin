Quick questions

Do I need to be home?

Nope. Just leave your empty cans at the curb on your scheduled day after the garbage truck comes. We clean them and put them back.

Do you use my water?

No — we bring our own and capture the dirty rinse water so nothing runs into your gutter or storm drain.

Is there a contract?

None. Recurring plans are month-to-month — pause, skip a visit, or cancel whenever you need to.

Which areas do you serve?
