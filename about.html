Family-owned, Fresno-grown, and a little too excited about a good before-and-after.

Squeaky Clean started with a simple gripe: dragging a hose into the side yard to half-clean a can that still smelled awful by Tuesday. There had to be a better way — so we built it for Fresno.

Anyone who's spent a July in the Central Valley knows what 100-degree heat does to a can with last week's chicken in it. We got our own rig, dialed in a hot-water system that actually disinfects, and started knocking on doors in our own neighborhood.

A few years in, we're still family-owned, still answering the phone ourselves, and still treat every cart like it's parked in front of our own house — because half the time, the next one practically is.
