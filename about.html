Our story

Born from one too many summer stink-ups

Anyone who's spent a July in the Central Valley knows what 100-degree heat does to a trash can with last week's chicken in it. We were tired of it. We looked around and saw that the folks who already had a truck and the gear were booked solid — clearly people wanted this. So we got our own rig, dialed in a hot-water system that actually disinfects, and started knocking on doors in our own neighborhood.

A few years in, we're still family-owned, still answering the phone ourselves, and still a little too excited about a before-and-after. We treat every cart like it's parked in front of our own house — because half the time, the next one practically is.
