About our company

A locally owned Fresno business built on doing one thing properly: keeping your bins genuinely clean.

Squeaky Clean Garbage Bin began with a simple problem: no homeowner should have to drag out a hose and breathe in months of buildup just to half-clean a can that still smells by mid-week.

Anyone who has spent a summer in the Central Valley knows what 100-degree heat does to a trash can. So we invested in a professional hot-water system that actually disinfects, and began serving our own neighborhood — one cart at a time.

Today we remain family-owned and operated. We answer the phone ourselves, show up when we say we will, and treat every property with the same care we'd give our own home.
